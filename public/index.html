Who We Are

More Than A Print Shop —
We're Family

At June's Tees & Things, we're more than just a print shop — we're a Chicago-based, Black-owned, women-owned business that brings your vision to life with passion and precision.

From custom screen printing and embroidery to DTF transfers and graphic design, we handle every step of the process with care. We pride ourselves on fast turnaround times, competitive pricing, and the kind of personal attention that makes every client feel like family.

Whether you need 1 shirt or 1,000, a quick event print or a fully branded uniform set — we're here for you.

Photo Requirements

Action Shot Photo Guide

The #1 rule: Original high-res files only — no screenshots, no social media downloads, no cropped phone photos.
For Life-Size Standees
  • ✓ Full-body photo from head to toe — no cropping at knees or feet
  • ✓ Plain, uncluttered background (solid color preferred)
  • ✓ Good lighting — avoid harsh shadows on the face
  • ✓ Action pose in jersey, uniform, or cap & gown
  • ✓ Minimum 2MB — JPG or PNG direct from camera (not from social media)
For Banners, Arches & Décor
  • ✓ Logos: vector file (SVG/AI/EPS) or high-res PNG with transparent background
  • ✓ School colors: hex codes or Pantone numbers if possible
  • ✓ Headshots: same rules — original, unedited, high-res
How to Submit
  • 1. Use the upload button in Section 7 of this form to upload directly
  • 2. JPG, PNG, or PDF — up to 20MB per file, up to 10 files
  • 3. If you have trouble uploading, note it in the Special Instructions field and we'll follow up within 2 hours

The Service

What Is Custom Embroidery?

Embroidery is the art of stitching designs, logos, text, and artwork directly into fabric using thread. Unlike printed designs, embroidery is three-dimensional — it has texture, depth, and a premium feel that can't be replicated by printing. It's the go-to choice for professional uniforms, corporate apparel, team gear, and anything that needs to look high-end.

How embroidery works

  • Your logo or design is converted into a digital stitch file (digitizing)
  • An embroidery machine maps out every stitch path, direction, and density
  • The garment is hooped and the machine stitches the design directly into the fabric
  • Finished pieces are trimmed, pressed, and quality checked
  • Embroidered designs are permanent, colorfast, and completely washable

Best for

  • Hats, caps, and beanies
  • Polo shirts and dress shirts
  • Jackets, hoodies, and outerwear
  • Work uniforms and staff apparel
  • Tote bags and accessories
  • Corporate gifts and branded merchandise
  • Sports team gear and coach apparel
  • Church and organization uniforms
Popular Uses

When to Choose Embroidery

Embroidery is the professional's choice. When you need your brand or name to look sharp, durable, and premium — whether on a $200 jacket or an everyday work polo — embroidery delivers results that printing simply can't match.

💼

Corporate & Business Uniforms

Employee uniforms, manager polos, reception desk apparel, service team jackets — embroidered logos make your team look cohesive, professional, and brand-consistent every single day.

🧢

Custom Embroidered Hats & Caps

Structured and unstructured caps, dad hats, trucker hats, beanies — embroidered headwear is one of the most popular branded merchandise items for businesses and organizations.

⚽

Sports Teams & Coaches

Team name and number embroidery on jerseys, coach polo shirts, team bags, and warm-up jackets. Durable enough to survive the entire season — and the washing machine.

🏥

Healthcare & Service Industries

Scrubs, lab coats, vests, and aprons with embroidered name tags, titles, and logos. Embroidery is required in many healthcare settings because it stays clean through industrial washing.

🎁

Corporate Gifts & Swag

Embroidered tote bags, branded zip-ups, company hats, and gift sets make impressive employee gifts, client appreciation packages, and conference giveaways.

⛪

Churches & Organizations

Ministry shirts, choir robes, volunteer vests, school uniforms, and club apparel. Embroidery looks uniform and professional across different garment styles and colors.

FAQs
Is there a digitizing fee?
Yes — a digitizing/conversion fee is mandatory for all embroidery orders unless you provide a workable stitch file (.DST, .PES, .EMB or similar). This fee covers converting your artwork into the stitch format the machine requires. We'll quote the digitizing fee before starting any work.
What's the minimum for embroidery?
We have no minimum on most embroidery items. Single hat orders are welcome, though the per-piece price decreases significantly at 6, 12, and 24+ pieces.
Can you embroider on hats I already own?
Yes, in many cases. Bring your hats in or ship them to us and we'll evaluate whether they're suitable for embroidery. Most structured and semi-structured caps work great.
How detailed can embroidered designs be?
Embroidery works best for logos, text, and bold shapes. Very fine details and gradients may need to be simplified. We'll review your design during the digitizing step and suggest any adjustments needed.
How long does embroidery take?
Standard turnaround is 1–2 weeks. Rush options are available. Once your design is digitized, reorders are even faster.

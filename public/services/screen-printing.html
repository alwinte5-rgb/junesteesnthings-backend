The Service

What Is Screen Printing?

Screen printing — also called silk screening — is a method of applying ink directly through a mesh stencil onto fabric. Each color in the design is printed as a separate layer, creating vivid, opaque prints that hold up wash after wash. It's the gold standard for high-volume custom apparel, event merchandise, team uniforms, and branded clothing.

How the process works

  • Your artwork is separated into individual color layers
  • A mesh screen is created for each ink color
  • Ink is pushed through each screen onto the garment in perfect alignment
  • Prints are heat-cured for permanent, wash-fast results
  • Finished garments are inspected, folded, and ready for pickup or delivery

Best for

  • Orders of 12 or more shirts
  • 2–6 spot color designs
  • Bold graphics and text-heavy artwork
  • Uniforms, team jerseys, and staff shirts
  • Event merch, concert tees, and festival apparel
  • Graduation shirts and senior class apparel
  • School spirit wear and athletic gear
  • Business promotional apparel and branded giveaways

Common Questions

Screen Printing FAQs

What's the minimum order for screen printing?
Our minimum for screen printing is typically 12 pieces per design. For smaller quantities (under 12), we recommend DTF transfers, which have no minimum and offer similar quality.
How long does screen printing take?
Standard turnaround is 2–3 weeks from approved proof. Rush 7-day delivery is available for most orders. Contact us to check current availability for rush orders.
What file format do you need for screen printing artwork?
Screen printing requires print-ready artwork in vector format (AI, EPS, or SVG). Your design should be complete and properly prepared before submitting. If your file needs significant cleanup or conversion to be print-ready, an artwork fee may apply — we'll quote it before doing any work.
Can you print on dark shirts?
Absolutely. Screen printing uses opaque inks that show up brilliantly on black, navy, dark grey, and any other dark garment color. We specialize in printing on dark fabric.
Do you supply the shirts or do I bring my own?
We supply blanks from top brands like Gildan, Bella+Canvas, Next Level, and more. If you have specific blanks you want printed, contact us to discuss your options.
Do you serve areas outside Chicago?
Yes! We serve all Chicago neighborhoods and surrounding suburbs including Evanston, Oak Park, Cicero, Harvey, Calumet City, and more. Shipping is available nationwide.

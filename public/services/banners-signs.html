FAQs

Banners & Signs FAQs

Are your outdoor banners weatherproof?
Yes. Our vinyl banners are printed on heavy-duty outdoor vinyl with UV-resistant inks and reinforced edges. They're designed to hold up in Chicago weather — wind, rain, and sun included.
Do yard signs come with stakes?
Yes — all yard signs include wire stakes for easy outdoor installation. Just push them into the ground and you're done.
How long does banner printing take?
Most banners and signs are ready within 3–5 business days. Rush 24–48 hour turnaround is available for an additional fee.
What file format do you need?
High-resolution PDF, AI, or PNG (300 DPI at print size) works best. For large format, we recommend vector files when possible. Don't have artwork? We'll design it for you.
Can I order a single sign?
Yes — there's no minimum for banners and signs. Single pieces are welcome.

What We Offer

Custom Banners & Signs in Chicago

From small yard signs to massive event backdrops, we produce sharp, full-color large-format printing on the right material for every job. Every size is available — we quote based on exactly what you need.

🪧

Vinyl Banners

Heavy-duty outdoor vinyl with UV-resistant inks and reinforced grommets. Weatherproof and built to last for indoor or outdoor use. Any size.

🌿

Coroplast Yard Signs

4mm and 10mm corrugated plastic UV-printed signs. Fluted for wire stakes. Perfect for graduation announcements, real estate, events, and political signage.

📸

Step-and-Repeat Backdrops

Full-color vinyl backdrops for photo booths, brand activations, and event photography. Grommets included. Any size — popular at 8'×8'.

🔄

Retractable Banner Stands

Trade show and event retractable stands with premium 8mil no-edge-curl printed banner. Collapses for easy transport. Great for promotions and displays.

🕸️

Mesh Banners

9oz coated polyester with 37% air perforation — designed for outdoor fences, construction sites, and high-wind environments. Wind passes through, your brand stays put.

🚗

Vehicle Magnets

30mil flexible vehicle magnets in full color. Goes on, comes off easily without damaging your vehicle's paint. Great for business vehicles and mobile advertising.

🪟

Window Graphics & Clings

Static cling vinyl for storefronts, one-way perforated window vinyl for privacy + visibility, and double-sided window graphics visible from both sides.

🧱

Wall Graphics

Removable low-tac adhesive wall graphics for indoor spaces. Great for pop-up shops, event decor, offices, and temporary brand displays.

👣

Floor Graphics

Anti-slip adhesive floor vinyl for smooth surfaces — tile, wood, and concrete. Eye-catching directional signage, branding, and event decor underfoot.

📋

Rigid Signs — PVC & Foam Core

3mm and 6mm smooth PVC and 3/16" foam board for indoor displays, menus, directional signs, trade shows, and event signage. Lightweight and professional.

🔩

Aluminum & Acrylic Signs

0.04"–0.08" metal aluminum signs and 3/16" acrylic for long-term indoor/outdoor use. Storefronts, real estate, offices, and permanent business signage.

🖼️

Posters & Backlit Prints

80# gloss poster printing for events, retail, and promotions. Backlit panels for lightbox frames. Sharp, vivid output for high-visibility indoor displays.

📐 Every product is available in fully custom sizes — just tell us your dimensions and we'll quote it.

FAQs

Banners & Signs FAQs

Are your outdoor banners weatherproof?
Yes. Our vinyl banners are printed on heavy-duty outdoor vinyl with UV-resistant inks and reinforced edges. They're designed to hold up in Chicago weather — wind, rain, and sun included.
Do yard signs come with stakes?
Yes — all yard signs include wire stakes for easy outdoor installation. Just push them into the ground and you're done.
How long does banner printing take?
Most banners and signs are ready within 3–5 business days. Rush 24–48 hour turnaround is available for an additional fee.
What file format do you need?
High-resolution PDF, AI, or PNG (300 DPI at print size) works best. For large format, we recommend vector files when possible. Don't have artwork? We'll design it for you.
Can I order a single sign?
Yes — there's no minimum for banners and signs. Single pieces are welcome.
What's the difference between all the sign materials?
Vinyl banners are best for large outdoor displays and events. Coroplast yard signs go on wire stakes in the ground. Mesh banners are for fences and windy outdoor spots. PVC and foam core are for indoor displays and trade shows. Aluminum and acrylic are for permanent or long-term signage. Vehicle magnets go on your car. Not sure? Tell us what you're trying to do and we'll recommend the right material.
What sizes do you offer?
Any size. There are no size restrictions — we quote custom dimensions. Tell us exactly what you need and we'll price it out for you.

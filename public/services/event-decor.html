What We Offer

Custom Event Decor & Party Favors

We create fully custom event decor and party favors using your photos, names, school colors, and designs. Every piece is made-to-order — nothing generic, nothing off the shelf. Your event will look exactly the way you imagined it.

FAQs

Event Decor FAQs

What photo quality do I need for standees and cutouts?
For best results, provide the highest resolution photo you have — ideally a clear, well-lit photo where the subject is the main focus. We'll let you know if the image won't print well before we proceed.
How tall are your life-size standees?
Our standees are produced up to 6 feet tall and printed on durable corroplast with a built-in easel stand. They're waterproof, UV-resistant, and can be used indoors or outdoors.
How many chip bags should I order?
For graduation parties, most customers order 24–48 chip bags. We offer set pricing for sets of 6 and 12. Mix and match flavors on request.
How far in advance should I order?
We recommend ordering 2–3 weeks before your event. Rush options are available for most items if you need something faster — contact us to check availability.
Can I see a proof before production?
Yes — you'll receive a digital proof for approval on every order before we print. We don't start production until you've approved the design.

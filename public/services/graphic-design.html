Common Questions

Graphic Design FAQs

Is artwork included with my print order?
Simple edits and predefined designs are included with your order. If your artwork requires major editing to meet print quality standards, an artwork fee may apply — June's Tees & Things makes that determination on a case-by-case basis and will always send a quote before proceeding.
What artwork format do I need to provide for screen printing?
Screen printing requires print-ready artwork in vector format (AI, EPS, or SVG). Your design should be complete and properly prepared before submitting. If your file needs conversion or major cleanup to be print-ready, an artwork fee may apply.
Do I own the design file?
You own the design files only if you purchase them as part of your order. File ownership is not automatic — it applies when you have paid for the design work. We'll confirm file delivery when applicable.
I have a low-quality image of my logo — can you fix it?
Yes. Vector conversion (redrawing your logo in scalable format) is available. Because this is major artwork work rather than a simple edit, a fee may apply. We'll review your file and send a quote if a conversion fee is needed before doing any work.
Can I get just the design file without printing?
Design-only projects are available. Contact us and we'll quote it based on the scope of work.

The Service

What Are DTF Transfers?

DTF stands for Direct to Film. It's a modern printing process where your design is printed in full color onto a special film, coated with heat-activated adhesive powder, and then transferred to fabric using a heat press. The result is a vivid, soft, durable print that works on virtually any material and any color garment — with zero minimums.

How DTF works

  • Your full-color design is printed onto special transfer film
  • Hot-melt adhesive powder is applied and cured onto the printed film
  • The transfer is pressed onto the garment at high heat and pressure
  • The film peels away, leaving a vibrant, flexible print bonded to the fabric
  • Prints are wash-fast, soft to the touch, and extremely durable

Best for

  • Orders of any size — even a single shirt
  • Photographic and full-color designs
  • Complex artwork with gradients and shadows
  • Polyester, nylon, and performance fabrics
  • Printing on dark and light garments equally
  • Small business launches and pop-up shops
  • Sample orders before a large screen print run
  • One-of-a-kind custom gifts and memorial shirts

Common Questions

DTF Transfer FAQs

Do DTF transfers wash well?
Yes. DTF transfers are extremely durable when properly applied. They're wash-fast and don't crack or peel when laundered according to care instructions (inside-out, cold wash, no bleach).
Can I order just one shirt with a DTF transfer?
Absolutely — there are no minimums for DTF. Single-piece orders are welcome and the price is affordable compared to other methods at low quantities.
What's the difference between DTF and sublimation?
Sublimation only works on white or light polyester fabrics. DTF works on any fabric color and any material — cotton, polyester, blends, nylon, and more. DTF is much more versatile.
Can you do gang sheets (multiple designs on one sheet)?
Yes! We offer gang sheet transfers — we arrange multiple designs on one film sheet to maximize efficiency and reduce per-design cost. Great for small businesses and Etsy sellers.
How long does a DTF order take?
Standard turnaround is 3–7 business days depending on quantity. Rush options are available. Single-design orders can often be completed in 1–2 days.
